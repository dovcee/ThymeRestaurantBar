Meet The Owner


  Nelli Coppin began her journery as a chef from the age of 5 in Linden, Guyana. Growing up with her Granny Lee gave her the 1st tool in culinary arts, Love. Setting up shop during rush hours or after a good lime, Granny Lee would take Nelli to provided the locals with nourishment that blessed their families to prosper. After graduating from Carnegie School of Home Economics, Nelli traveled thousand of miles to New York to spread the culture of Back Home Cooking. From morning to night the Nelli put love, sweat, & tears into buliding her catering business. Never straying away from her roots. After winning the 2007 Best West Indian Cusine Award, Nelli Coppin opened her 1st restaurant naming it Thyme©. A mordern yet authentic twist to West Indian dishes peaks niegbors & tourist to get a taste of culture while sharing the love of vibes. Why Thyme? Well Granny Lee always said:
 
Cooking requires taking time & putting in thyme.
